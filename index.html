ABOUT THE JOURNAL

Everest Advances in Science and Technologies (EAST) is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal published by Everest College. The journal is envisioned as a dynamic platform for disseminating high-quality research and innovative ideas in the broad fields of engineering, science, and technology. EAST seeks to encourage collaboration, knowledge sharing, and academic excellence among researchers, professionals, and students.

All published articles in EAST will be assigned a unique Digital Object Identifier (DOI), ensuring global accessibility and long-term citation. The journal aspires to be recognized in leading indexing databases, including Nepal Journals Online (NepJOL), thereby enhancing the visibility of published work.
